## 1. DEEP DIVE: The New Labour Codes Revolution

### What Changed on November 21, 2025

India consolidated 29 labor laws into **4 Labour Codes**:

1. **Code on Wages 2019** â€” Redefines "wages" (basic must be â‰¥50% of CTC)
2. **Code on Social Security 2020** â€” Unified PF, ESI, gratuity framework
3. **Industrial Relations Code 2020** â€” Strike rules, layoff procedures
4. **OSH Code 2020** â€” Safety standards, now apply to 10+ employee companies

### The Compliance Chaos

- **CTC Restructuring Required:** If basic salary < 50% of gross, companies must restructure. This affects take-home pay calculations for millions.
- **Digital Registers Mandatory:** Paper muster rolls â†’ digital attendance
- **Unified Compliance Portal:** Single digital return instead of 20+ separate filings
- **Gig Worker Coverage:** For the first time, gig and platform workers get social security

### Why This Creates Opportunity

Every SMB in India needs to:
- Restructure salary components
- Switch to digital attendance
- Update appointment letters
- Recalculate PF/gratuity liabilities

**The 6-month window (Nov 2025 - May 2026) is the land grab.**
